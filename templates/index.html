A library is not only an institution that gathers knowledge, but also one that makes it possible to create and add to the body of knowledge through experience. The concept of the library as a meeting place or a hands-on creative space is based on the intuition that when a library is thriving—lively and full of activity—then something unexpected, ambitious, or transformative may take place.

"Who Needs a Librarian and Cataloger When You Have Google and Internet? Well, Who Needs a Teacher When You Have Wikipedia? And, Who Needs a Doctor When You Have WebMD? Just as the Wikipedia Doesn't Replace the Teacher, and WebMD Doesn’t Replace the Doctor, In the Same Way, Google Search and Internet Doesn’t Replace the Librarian and Cataloger."

Salman Haider
